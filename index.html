Dr. Jack Polifka

Welcome to my academic website! My name is Jack Polifka and I am a Clinical Assistant Professor at Boise State University in the Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies major since 2021. My primary role is teaching courses that include full-stack web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MySQL), the Internet of Things, and career readiness. My research interests include assessment of multiple representations in STEM courses and web development education.
